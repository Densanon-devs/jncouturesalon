Jill George is the owner of JN Couture and a seasoned salon professional with over 20 years of experience in the beauty industry. As an extension specialist and master colorist, Jill has built a reputation for delivering customized, high-quality results that enhance each client's natural beauty.

Her approach is both professional and laid-back, creating a comfortable, welcoming space where clients can relax and feel confident they're in expert hands. Jill is passionate about helping every person who sits in her chair feel like the most beautiful version of themselves.

As a proud mom of five, Jill understands the importance of balancing real life with self-care. She brings that perspective into her work, offering services that not only look amazing but fit seamlessly into her clients' lifestyles.

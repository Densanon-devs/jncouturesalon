Haircuts & Styling

Precision haircuts and finishing styles tailored to you.

Every haircut at JN Couture starts with a real conversation. We listen to what you want, look at what your hair is doing now, and recommend a shape that works with your texture, density, and lifestyle — not against it.

From classic cuts and trims to face-framing layers, modern shags, and textured bobs, our stylists tailor every appointment to you. Each haircut includes a personalized consultation, shampoo, and blow-dry finish.

What's Included

  • Personalized consultation to understand your goals
  • Shampoo and conditioning service
  • Precision cut tailored to your hair texture and face shape
  • Blow-dry finish and styling tips for at home

Great For

  • Refreshing your shape with a clean trim
  • A modern restyle — long layers, shag, lob, or pixie
  • Growing out a previous cut without losing polish
  • Specialty cuts for curly, fine, or thick hair

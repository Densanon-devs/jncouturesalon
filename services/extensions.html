Hair Extensions

Master extension work — hand-tied and tape-in, 100% human hair.

Extensions are Jill George's signature service. With over 20 years in the industry as a certified extension specialist, Jill installs hand-tied wefts and tape-in extensions using 100% human hair, color-matched and blended so the result looks like it grew out of your head.

Extensions are a process — we start with a consultation to discuss the look you want, the maintenance you can commit to, and the method that will be best for your hair type and density. From there, we order custom-matched hair and book your installation.

Extension Methods

  • Hand-tied wefts (sewn onto a beaded foundation)
  • Tape-in extensions
  • Custom-blended for length, density, and color
  • 100% human hair only — never synthetic

Great For

  • Adding length without growing out
  • Adding fullness to fine or thinning hair
  • Adding dimension or color without lightening your own hair
  • Filling in after a haircut you want to recover from

Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

With proper care, hand-tied extensions are typically moved up every 6 to 8 weeks and the hair itself can last 6 to 12 months. Tape-ins are moved up every 6 to 8 weeks. Lifespan depends on care, products, and how often you heat-style.

Installed correctly and maintained on schedule, no — we use methods that distribute weight evenly and avoid stress on individual strands. Aftercare and following the move-up schedule are key.

Cost varies significantly by length, density, and method. We provide a detailed quote at consultation, including the hair, install, and ongoing maintenance schedule, so there are no surprises.

We will give you a complete care plan: sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, a recommended brush, a sleeping routine, and how to wash and heat-style without compromising the install.

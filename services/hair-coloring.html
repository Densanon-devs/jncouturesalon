Hair Coloring

All-over color, root touch-ups, gloss, and color corrections.

Color is where artistry meets chemistry, and we take both seriously. From a quick root refresh to a full color transformation or correction, our colorists pair your goals with formulations that deliver vibrant, long-lasting results while protecting the integrity of your hair.

Every color service starts with a consultation. We assess your current condition, your maintenance preferences, and your inspiration photos before we mix a single bowl.

Color Services We Offer

  • All-over permanent or demi-permanent color
  • Root touch-ups and color refresh
  • Color corrections (pulling out unwanted tones, going lighter or darker safely)
  • Glosses and toners for shine and tonal adjustment
  • Gray blending and full gray coverage
  • Creative and fashion color (consultation required)

Great For

  • Refreshing faded color or grown-out roots
  • Going darker, richer, or warmer for the season
  • Fixing a color from another salon or at-home box
  • Adding shine and depth without committing to a full color

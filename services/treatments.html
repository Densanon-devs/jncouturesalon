Hair Treatments

Deep conditioning, keratin smoothing, bond repair, and scalp care.

Healthy hair is the foundation of any great cut or color. Our treatment menu is designed to repair, protect, and refresh — whether your hair is thirsty for moisture, needs structural bond repair after lightening, or just deserves a luxurious reset.

Treatments can be added on to almost any appointment, or booked on their own as a standalone service.

Treatments We Offer

  • Deep conditioning and moisture masks
  • Bond-repair treatments (rebuilds disulfide bonds after lightening)
  • Keratin smoothing services for frizz reduction and manageability
  • Scalp exfoliation and treatments
  • Color-protection finishing treatments

Great For

  • Hair that feels dry, brittle, or rough at the ends
  • Frequent heat-styling or sun damage
  • Coarse, frizz-prone hair that wants daily ease
  • Itchy, flaky, or buildup-prone scalps

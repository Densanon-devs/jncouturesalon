Salon Policies

JN Couture Salon operates by appointment only to ensure every client receives dedicated time and attention. Call us at (208) 762-4667 or email jncouturesalon@gmail.com to schedule your visit.

Come with clean, dry hair unless your stylist specifies otherwise. Bring any inspiration photos you have in mind. For color appointments, avoid washing your hair the day of — a little natural oil actually helps protect your scalp during processing.

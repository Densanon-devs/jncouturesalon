Blowouts

Smooth, bouncy, or textured — polished styling on demand.

A great blowout is a quick reset. In about 45 minutes you walk out with smooth, polished, camera-ready hair and a finish that holds for days.

Whether you want sleek and straight, soft waves, voluminous bounce, or a textured tousle, our stylists know how to coax the right shape out of your hair using the products and tools that suit your texture.

Blowout Styles We Love

  • Sleek and straight
  • Voluminous round-brush bounce
  • Soft waves and curls
  • Textured, undone, lived-in

Great For

  • Looking polished without the daily styling effort
  • Date nights, photo days, and special events
  • A bridal hair preview before the big day
  • Pairing with a quick scalp refresh between color appointments

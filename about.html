Our Story

Get to know the people and philosophy behind JN Couture Salon.

Est. 2007

A Passion for Beautiful Hair

JN Couture Salon was founded by Jill George with a simple belief: everyone deserves to feel confident and beautiful when they leave the salon chair. What started as a vision for a personalized, boutique hair experience has grown into one of Northern Idaho's most trusted salons.

With nearly two decades behind the chair, Jill has built a team of talented stylists who share her commitment to quality, artistry, and genuine client care. Our studio in Hayden offers a relaxed, upscale atmosphere where you can unwind while our skilled team works their magic.

We invest in ongoing education and training so our team stays current with the latest techniques, trends, and products. From lived-in color to flawless extensions, we bring a blend of artistry and technical skill to every service.

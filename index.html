Why Choose JN Couture Salon?

Founded in 2007 by Jill George, JN Couture Salon has been a trusted name in Northern Idaho hair care for nearly two decades. Our boutique studio in Hayden offers a relaxing, upscale experience where every appointment is personal.

We believe great hair starts with great relationships. Every visit begins with a one-on-one consultation so we can understand your goals, lifestyle, and hair history before we ever pick up the scissors.
